About Us

Welcome to Youtube Upload Plateform, where we simplify video management. Our platform allows video editors to upload content, which then waits for user approval. Once approved, the video is automatically uploaded to YouTube.

Features

Video Upload by Editors

Editors can easily upload videos to our platform. These videos are securely stored and prepared for review.

Approval Workflow

After uploading, videos await approval from users. Users are notified to review and approve or reject the video.

Automated YouTube Upload

Once a video is approved by the user, our platform handles the upload process to YouTube, ensuring a seamless experience.

Real-Time Notifications

Get real-time notifications about the status of video uploads and approvals, keeping you informed throughout the process.
